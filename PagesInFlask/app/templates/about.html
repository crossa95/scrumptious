About

The purpose of PMT is to give software developers a packaged workflow tool. PMT combines a chat system and a Kanban board function that allows for convenient communications between team members during Scrum Sprints. Similar to other websites, members will need an account and will require an invitation to join projects that they did not make themselves. PMT uses Javascript and HTML for the front end and Flask for the back end. A SQLAlchemy database stores all of the information needed to keep track of who you are, what projects you have, message histories and kanban boards. The convenince of having something akin to Slack and Trello combined into a single site and account makes KanBan+Chat an excellent option for your team! Minmize the need to remember passwords, create more efficent team management, and accelerate productivity with KanBan+Chat.
